My Blog

Kapoo production: Pratical Traning 2567/3

April - June 2025

During the internship at Kapoo Production (The Duck Gen Co., Ltd.), I held the position of a 3D Animator Intern. My main responsibilities encompassed creating and revising 3D animation assets, cleaning up motion capture data, developing 3D models for use in event-related projects, and collaborating closely with the animation and production teams. Key tasks included animating character models, designing assets for a live gacha event, and producing VTuber-style speed ramp animation clips.

This internship allowed me to build a strong foundation in 3D animation production, including technical skills in Blender and Unity, and hands-on experience with the animation pipeline typical in digital content and event productions. I also developed soft skills such as time management, teamwork, and adapting to fast-paced, real-world production environments. Regular feedback and collaborative assignments deepened my understanding of both the art and technical requirements of industry-level animation work.

The internship was highly suitable for computer engineering students with an interest in computer graphics, animation, and creative technology. It provided a practical blend of software development, algorithmic problem solving (especially in handling animation curves and motion data), and multidisciplinary teamwork. All of which are valuable competencies in the modern field of computer engineering. The real-world exposure to production workflows, team collaboration, and industry-standard tools makes this internship highly recommended for future cohorts seeking a career at the intersection of technology and digital media.
